Metadata

Metadata is a very useful thing to use when posting videos. This helps your videos show up farther up the search quaury, in turn drawing more viewers to your channel. Metadata consists of Title, Tags, and discription. You want to get very popular tags, name your title something that would sound apealing to your viewers(Ohterwise known as click bait), and a description containing key words from your title and tags weaved into a acurate description to your video.
